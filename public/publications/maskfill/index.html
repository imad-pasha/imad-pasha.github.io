maskfill: A robust and simple method to infill masks in astronomical images

Pieter van Dokkum & Imad Pasha PASP (accepted).

Astronomical images often have regions with missing or unwanted information, such as bad pixels, bad columns, cosmic rays, masked objects, or residuals from imperfect model subtractions. We present a deterministic, robust, and interpretable Python package to inpaint these pixels while retaining valuable statistical information inferred from the data. (more).
